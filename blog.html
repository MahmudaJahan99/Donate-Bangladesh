Important FAQ

What is the purpose of this website?

This website is created to support flood victims in Bangladesh by making it easy for people to contribute financially. Bangladesh experiences devastating floods almost every year, displacing thousands of families and causing significant damage to infrastructure and livelihoods. Our mission is to act as a bridge between donors and the affected communities, ensuring that donations are channeled into the most impactful relief efforts.

Where does my donation go?

Every donation we receive is directed towards immediate and long-term relief efforts for those affected by the floods in Bangladesh. This includes distributing essential items like food, water, medicine, and shelter, as well as supporting recovery projects, such as rebuilding homes and infrastructure. We ensure that your donation reaches the areas that need it most by working with local NGOs and relief organizations on the ground. Our team also regularly monitors and evaluates how funds are spent to maximize their impact and ensure transparency in all transactions.

Can I donate non-monetary items like clothes or food?

At this time, we are primarily focusing on monetary donations because they allow us to respond to emergencies faster and more efficiently. Sending physical goods, such as clothes or food, involves logistics challenges like shipping, storage, and distribution. Monetary donations give us the flexibility to purchase items locally, which is often quicker and more cost-effective. However, we may organize non-monetary donation drives in the future, so stay tuned for updates if you would like to contribute in other ways.
